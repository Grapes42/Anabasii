Anabasii

Simple large file sharer

What does Anabasii mean?

Anabasii gets its name from Ancient Roman messengers who delivered messages over long distances on chariots. It comes from the Greek word αναβασις (adscensus) meaning "mounting".

Anabasii were regularily used by the Romans and the Greeks around the time of Augustus. The changed horses often to maintain a fast rate of travel.

This is the reasoning for the name "Anabasii". It is designed to be a fast and simple file sharing (messaging) system.
